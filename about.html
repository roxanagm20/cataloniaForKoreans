About Me



Hello and welcome to my webpage, Catalonia for Koreans!

I have decided to create this website to help Koreans who are interested in visiting Catalonia but don't have enough information to do so.

In this website, you will be able to find different places to enjoy while visiting Catalonia.

This is also my final project for my university, so any sort of feedback will be welcomed.

Thank you so much for taking the time to check this website, and hope you enjoy your stay in Catalonia!
